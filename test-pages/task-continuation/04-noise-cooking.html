Ingredients

This classic Italian pasta dish requires simple ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper

Cooking Instructions

Follow these steps for perfect carbonara:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy
  3. Mix egg yolks with grated cheese
  4. Combine everything off heat to avoid scrambling
  5. Add lots of black pepper

The secret to authentic carbonara is the creamy sauce made from eggs and cheese, not cream!

Pro Tips for Italian Cooking

When making pasta carbonara:

  • Never add cream to traditional carbonara
  • Use fresh Pecorino Romano, not Parmesan
  • Reserve some pasta water for the sauce
  • Work quickly to maintain the right temperature for cooking

Authentic Italian cuisine relies on quality ingredients and simple cooking techniques.
